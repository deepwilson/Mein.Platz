    My time I knew sss'about to end

    His gaze below engrossed in scrawl
    I saw my Ray of hope befall

    The one who has not sinned - assault!!
    The stones that were ..
    raised to destroy

    Wiped out their ploy
    
    Soul impure
    Loathe endured
    No more sorrow.. I will endure !
