About Me


My name is Oluwaleke Umar Yusuf and I'm a mechanical engineer. I'm a highly resourceful mechanical engineer with over three years of industry experience in construction and telecommunications. I'm from Nigeria, living in Egypt and currently studying Robotics, Control and Smart Systems at the American University In Cairo. You can find me on GitHub or on Twitter.

On this page you can find some information about me and my blog. My blog posts ate mainly geared towards detail my experiences and development with Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Gesture Recognition as I work on my masters. However, there are also posts about topics that I'm most interested in and my opinions or reviews of books I've read.
